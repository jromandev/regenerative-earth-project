## Code of Conduct

The Regenerative Earth Project is committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for all contributors.

This project is guided by the belief that technology should strengthen communities and ecosystems rather than exploit them.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Engage in thoughtful and evidence-based dialogue
- Focus on solutions and collaborative problem-solving
- Respect cultural knowledge and local practices

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on race, gender, nationality, or belief
- Dismissal or disrespect toward indigenous or local knowledge
- Promotion of exploitative, militarized, or harmful uses of the platform
- Any behavior that undermines the mission of ecological regeneration and community empowerment

---

## Ethical Alignment

Because this project addresses land, resources, and communities, ethical responsibility is essential.

Contributors must respect that the platform:

- Is humanitarian in purpose
- Must not be used for exploitation or harm
- Must remain transparent and accountable

---

## Enforcement

Project stewards and maintainers are responsible for upholding this code of conduct.

Contributors who violate these principles may have their contributions rejected or access to the project limited.

---

## Our Commitment

We are building not just software, but a collaborative environment grounded in respect, responsibility, and stewardship.

Everyone participating shares responsibility for protecting that environment.
